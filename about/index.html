About Me

Hi! My name is Mouneim Ettouhami. I am a physicist by training, specializing in Condensed Matter Theory. A list of my physics publications can be found here.

These days, I am a data enthusiast. My favorite tools are Excel, Python and its various data science libraries (Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, scikit-learn), SQL in its various flavors (including big data engines like Apache Hive and Impala), as well as Apache Spark.

I am originally from Morocco. During the course of my studies, I spent time in France, first at Université de Provence in Marseille and then at Université Joseph Fourier in Grenoble. I then moved to the United States, and earned a PhD in Physics from the University of Colorado at Boulder. After holding two Postdoctoral positions, first at the University of Florida at Gainesville and then at the University of Toronto, I now live in the Toronto area, in the beautiful province of Ontario, Canada. In the summer months, I enjoy running, biking and kayaking.

If you enjoyed browsing through the projects in this portfolio and would like to ask a question or make a comment, please don’t hesitate to give me a shout. My email address is (ettouhami at gmail dot com).
